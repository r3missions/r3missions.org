About R3

R3 is an Adventist mission think tank that seeks to combine biblical innovative approaches to spiritual awakening with traditional forms of evangelism.

What we do?

Create opportunities for adventist youth to be mobilized into meaningful action for Christ.

Missions

Service projects tailored to specific mission fields that integrate teaching, healing, and gospel ministries that seek to mirror Christ's.

Media

Capturing and promoting ministry in various media forms to reveal God's character and work in our present time.

Music

Innovating ideas for how vocalists, instrumentalists, composers, and songwriters can utilize their Christ-given gifts for missions.

To love Him, the infinite, omniscient One, with the whole strength, and mind, and heart means the highest development of every power.

— Ellen White —

Leadership

Our culturally diverse team are Young, Experienced, and Passionate about finishing God's work in this generation.

Projects

Our projects seek to be creative, fresh, bold and biblical, but always thought through carefully, prayerfully, collaboratively and executed with patience.

Missions

R3 is collaborating with Spicer Memorial College, Pune Adventist Hospital, Moore SDA Church and more to plan 4 missions in 2014.

What we offer
  • Fundraising
  • Pune, India: January 7–21
  • Oklahoma: April 17–May 30
  • Middle East: Sept 22–Dec 16

Media

R3 is exploring traditional, photo, and video journaling of mission experiences across the globe along-side an online training platform and national geographic-esque mission magazine.

What we offer
  • Media Journaling Project
  • Application Development
  • Mission Photo Journalism
  • Mission Video Journalism
  • Mission Graphic Design

Music

R3 endeavors to create resources, opportunities, and networks of musicianaries across the globe in order to sound the note of God's love through the heavenly gift of music.

What we offer
  • GYC Orlando Concert
  • Music on a Mission
  • Adventist Recording Studio
  • Musicianary Network
  • Host an R3 Concert
