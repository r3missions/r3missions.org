Project Description

In December 2011, Michel Lee the Vice President of Resources asked me to join her team for GYC. I began with editing articles, program booklets, and the like. After the first GYC Europe, I started writing more articles and coordinating with wider networks to publish abroad the powerful things God was (and still is) doing through GYC. After covering the Seattle GYC conference, a non-denominational Christian magazine, Rejuvenate, contacted GYC to republish a reflective article of mine. I also loved working with Tamyra Horst to cover the GYC INTERmission in Allentown, PA in June 2013. This coming year, I will be mentoring new journalism interns.
—Callie Williams
