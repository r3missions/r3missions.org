Project Description

After canvassing with ExCEL in summer of 2011, the Michigan Conference hired me to Biblework with both Detroit Metro SDA church and the ExCEL program. Using the Bible study interests gathered by the canvassers, I Bibleworked all across southeastern Michigan. I worked with Pastor Jim Howard and Pastor Joe Reeves, who mentored me, supplied my Bible study literature, and oversaw the integration of the incoming Bible worker after I left. I also served as a ground leader of the ExCEL program several times a week, gave devotional talks for and mentored the canvassers.
—Callie Williams

Project Details

Partners: Michigan Conference of Seventh-day Adventists, Detroit Metro Seventh-day Adventist church, and ExCEL (Excellence through Canvassing, Evangelism, and Lifestyle)

Date: June–August 2012
