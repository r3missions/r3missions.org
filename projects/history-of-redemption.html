Project Description

Beneath the surface of Lucifer’s claim, that God is unfair and unjust, was his resistance and refusal to accept Christ as being God Himself. He wanted the glory that God the Father was giving to God the Son. Beneath the surface, the divinity of Christ was in question.

The History of Redemption, the study of past events pertaining to the redemption of man, is the stage upon which the character of Satan, God the Father, and Christ become clear. God is fair, God is just and Christ is even more deserving of His glory.

This controversy began long ago in the courts of heaven and today rages in our hearts. Before men and angels, a decision must be made. To whom will you give your whole heart?

This concert, a combination of scripture, powerful quotes from Christian authors, and song, is designed to call its listeners to a decision. What will you do with Jesus?
—Candis Braxton

Musicians

Yajaira Adorno is a native of Carolina, Puerto Rico. She’s a graduate of Universidad Adventista de las Antillas (Adventist University of the West Indies) majoring in biology. She is a passionate and vibrant young woman who loves music, especially singing, playing sports and keeping fit in the gym.

Candis Braxton is a mother of two and a wife of one. After being asked to sing for an evangelistic series while a freshman in college, she decided to give her life to Christ. “I sang the appeal song every night which meant I had to listen to all 14 messages. Everyone was shocked when I answered the call for baptism. I think they all thought I was already a member of the SDA church but I wasn’t.” Since making that decision she has also committed to using her voice to share the gospel of Christ.

James Ervin II was born and raised in Nacogdoches, TX but now resides in Ann Arbor, Michigan. He is a Bio Pre-Med student at Washtenaw Community College and serves as a local Elder at both the Ann Arbor Seventh-day Adventist Church in Ann Arbor and Campus HOPE Church at the University of Michigan. When asked to describe himself he replied, “I am a disciple of Jesus Christ.”

Sebastien Henri-Saturné grew up in Port-au-Prince, Haiti. He is currently pursuing a Masters of Divinity at the Andrews University Theological Seminary. He enjoys reading, listening music, spending time with his family and friends and has passion for sharing the Gospel.

Chayah Miranda (kai-yah) is a Christian Pop artist, best known for her sweet yet powerful voice. Performing all over the U.S., and throughout her native San Francisco Bay Area, she has won numerous awards for her vocals as well as her songwriting.

Before becoming a Christian artist, Chayah had her mind set on becoming a Pop R&B artist. While attending Berklee College of Music where she studied voice and performance, Chayah faced many obstacles and trials that brought her to reflect on what mattered most in her life. It was in early 2010 when she had to fight for her life and found herself praying to God for healing. Faithfully as God would have it, Chayah felt God's love and healing power. It was in that moment when she fully surrendered her life to Christ and made the decision to follow Jesus. Chayah has continued to follow Jesus and in doing so, she found her true calling...to serve God through her music and to spread the Gospel of good news to all who don't know Christ.

Chayah’s debut full-length album “No Other Way,” recorded in Nashville with producer Josh Robinson—who has worked with Britt Nicole, Brandon Heath, and Natalie Grant—will be released March 2014. The first single “Higher” off of this upcoming album is now available on iTunes.
