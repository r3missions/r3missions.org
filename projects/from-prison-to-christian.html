Project Description

It is a passion of mine to film people's testimonies as a witnessing tool to show the power of Christ to transform a soul no matter what lifestyle or experience they have been through. Prison was the last place Marcus Williams thought he would find God. His life revolved around the drug scene and the fast paced lifestyle that came with it but in spite of the activities of his life, God found a way to knock on the door of his heart and show him a purpose for his life that was bigger than he had ever imagined. This life testimony was filmed on location in Wolverhampton UK close to where Marcus grew up and lives.
