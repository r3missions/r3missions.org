Project Description

1 year ago, Moore, Oklahoma was devastated by an immense tornado that left large portions of its community in shambles. However, several organizations and churches stepped up to meet the enormous need.  Hope Adventist Fellowship Church kept their disaster relief going longer than most in the city. R3 teamed up with the Hope Church to continue bringing hope and encouragement to those still recovering in the Moore community.
