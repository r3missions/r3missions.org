Project Description

In August this year, Sebastien Braxton and Clive Coutet, the President and R3 Media Director, joined the Karengata SDA church for its annual camp meeting. It was a powerful and life changing experience for the R3 team, the Karengata SDA Church, the Victory SDA Choir, and the community of Nairobi. It was wonderful to end the camp meeting with the baptism and re-baptism of several youth and young adults.
