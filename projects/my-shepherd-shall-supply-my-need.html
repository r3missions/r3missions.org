Project Description

Based upon the first words of David's Psalm 23, this song really articulates many of my own personal prayers and praises. God has been faithful to me and my family, providing on so many fronts. This song is my praise of thanksgiving to Christ and my encouragement to others who may be waiting our Shepherd.
—Candis Braxton
