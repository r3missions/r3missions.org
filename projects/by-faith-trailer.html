Project Description

At GYC Louisville in 2009, I was approached by a good friend of mine, Foley Ibidapo, who worked with Affiliate Media at the time. He had planned to do a mini-documentary on a young man who backed out last minute. With the equipment present and the most influential adventist youth convention on the horizon, he asked if he could follow me around while speaking, living, and ministering. Afterwards, a concept emerged in regards to preaching being an act of faith. It was wonderful collaborating with Foley and the AFM team.
