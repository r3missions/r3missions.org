Project Description

GYC has always and continues to present the most influential youth event in the global Adventist church. But GYC also goes beyond the conference into missions into unentered areas, evangelism seminars, and even a conference in Europe. One of the recent projects regards the Great Controversy. This book singlehandedly brought me from staunch disregard for Christianity to a daily submission to its principles, purpose and person. Michel Lee, GYC Vice-President Resources asked me to help write a back cover summary for such a momentous work. After a couple drafts and conversations, this what we ended with. I also like Brandon Schroeder's design for the cover. Get a copy at www.thecontroversy.org.
