Project Description

This medley encapsulates the Christian’s experience in its beginning as well as when we are seeking spiritual renewal. Compelled by the desire to be a Christian, we come as we are to Jesus; and when we see Him, our prayer is to be like Him, and this can only be accomplished by surrendering all to Him.
—Candis Braxton
