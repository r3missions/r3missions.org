Project Description

A week before the mission flight to El Salvador, I was approached by the chaplain of Shenandoah Valley Academy to join. A student had backed out, leaving the chaplain with a fully paid ticket, and he urged me to come as a leader and translator. After arriving in San Juan Opico, I taught 3-6 grades Math and English throughout the week at ECAS. I spoke for Sabbath Divine Service at the end of our week of prayer, and I helped teach high school Bible class. Most memorable, I was able to form meaningful relationships with the locals and witness God work in and through us.
—Callie Williams

Project Details

Partners: Shenandoah Valley Academy (SVA) and Escuela de Capacitación Adventista Salvadoreña (ECAS)
