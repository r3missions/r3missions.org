Project Description

This summer, I was honored to join to AFOCE-Europe program and the Von Maurs in Darmstadt, Germany for a course on Campus Ministries and an Evangelistic Series in the evening. The series was an apologetic approach to serious questions that many in the secular world have. I was thankful to meet with an asian student one afternoon regarding hell and what happens after death. The clarity warmed his soul and he began inviting his friends to the series. Candis also sang the appeal songs in the evening which everyone broke out their smartphones to record. Powerful experience.
